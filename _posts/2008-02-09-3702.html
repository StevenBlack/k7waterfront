SOME BRIGHT SPACE STATION TRANSITS are expected over four consecutive evenings around dinnertime on February 12, 13, 14, and 15th.

The station will appear brighter than usual not just because of its orbital track. Currently Space Shuttle Atlantis is attached, and the station will soon be made slightly larger since the Columbus Science Laboratory is being added as part of this shuttle mission.

The table above, and the diagrams of the February 12th transit below, come from heavens-above.com, (free registration required) which is one of the really great resources on the web.

For viewers in Kingston, the Tuesday February 12th transit will just nick Polaris at 18:51:27.
