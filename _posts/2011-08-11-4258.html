FUNNY, ISN'T IT, that we're never told about actual participation in the 1000 Islands Poker run?

Earlier in August Kingston This Week predicted 150 boats which would have made this year's edition the biggest ever by streets.

About the event itself, The Brockville Recorder & Times reports "more than 60 boats took part.

As far as we know, local mainstream media had nothing to report after the event. The Whig Standard didn't run any Poker-Run related story, at least not online, following the event.
