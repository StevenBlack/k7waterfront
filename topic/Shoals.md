###Charity Shoal###

**Notes on Charity Shoal:** It's a crater.

* Diameter: ~1 Km,
* Location: Lake Ontario, Canada N 44 02' W 76 29' (Holleford Cater: N 44 28' W 76 38'
* See http://www.ottawa.rasc.ca/astronomy/earth_craters/possible_craters/
