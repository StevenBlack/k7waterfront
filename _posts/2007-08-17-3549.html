CORK BLOGGING: Here are links to competitors, volunteers, and spectators blogging about CORK. This beats the Kingston Whig Standard, which has had almost zero coverage so far, and the CORK website, which has a "News" link with no recent news to be found there.

  • Andrew Campbell, who recently won a gold medal for the USA in the Laser class at the Pan-Am Games: Monday Morning Tactician: 13 August 2007 about the importance of long-view weather checking before a competition like CORK.
  • John Pearce, a US Olympic sailing hopeful (he placed 4th at US Laser Nationals), posted an item titled Training Camp in Kingston on Wednesday.
  • Jenny, a CORK volunteer, posted an item titled CORK Sailing yesterday. It sounds like Jenny would love to do more actual sailing. Reading downward, she also volunteered for the Kingston Triathlon. Jenny's evidently a giver.
  • Monika's Adventures has an August 11th post titled CORK, Canadian Olympic Regatta Kingston. Monica also had a recent post titled International Sailing in Kingston about volunteering for the ISAF Youth World Championships in July.
